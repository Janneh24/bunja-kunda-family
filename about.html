About the Bunja Kunda Family

The Bunja Kunda family in Gunjur traces its roots back to Madi Gassama Janneh, recognized as the patriarch of the Janneh family in Gunjur. His son, Karim Janneh, had a son named Bunja Janneh (the first), who fathered several children: Kebba Ensa Janneh, Karamo Mustapha Janneh, Fa Lang Janneh, Kaddy Janneh, and Kani Janneh.
