About Us

The Bunja Kunda family in Gunjur traces its roots back to Madi Gassama Janneh, the patriarch of the Janneh family in Gunjur. His son, Karim Janneh, had a son named Bunja Janneh (the first), who fathered several children: Kebba Ensa Janneh, Karamo Mustapha Janneh, Fa Lang Janneh, Kaddy Janneh, and Kani Janneh.

Fa Lang Janneh had four sons: Fan Famara Janneh, Fa Fansu Janneh, Bunja Janneh (the second), and Fa Lankoi Janneh. Fa Fansu Janneh had five children: Fa Kemo, Fa Karamo, Fa Almameh, Fa Bunja, and Nfansu. Bunja Janneh (2) became the father of Basay, Kemo, Malang, and Omar.

We are committed to celebrating our heritage, maintaining our family bonds, and supporting one another as Bunja Kunda family members in Gunjur and beyond.
