About Us

The Bunja Kunda family in Gunjur traces its roots back to Madi Gassama Janneh, who is recognized as the patriarch of the Janneh family in Gunjur. His son, Karim Janneh, had a son named Bunja Janneh (the first), who fathered several children: Kebba Ensa Janneh, Karamo Mustapha Janneh, Fa Lang Janneh, Kaddy Janneh, and Kani Janneh.

Kebba Ensa Janneh had two children: Malang Jai Janneh and Sutay Janneh. Malang Jai Janneh became the father of Fan Mbomo (Momodou), Ansumana, Fa Alagie, Baba, and Mba Giki Janneh. Sutay Janneh was the mother of Karamo Sutay Touray of Gunjur Foday Kunda.

Fa Lang Janneh had four sons: Fan Famara Janneh, Fa Fansu Janneh, Bunja Janneh (the second), and Fa Lankoi Janneh. Fa Fansu Janneh had five children: Fa Kemo, Fa Karamo, Fa Almameh, Fa Bunja, and Nfansu. Bunja Janneh (the second) became the father of Basay, Kemo, Malang, and Omar.

We are committed to preserving our heritage, celebrating our family bonds, and supporting one another as members of the Bunja Kunda family in Gunjur and beyond.
